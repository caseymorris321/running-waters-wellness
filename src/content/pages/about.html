About Me

Running Waters Wellness

Hello! My name is Jeff Waters and I am an herbalist and wellness consultant. My job is to guide you toward the best version of you that you can be! Like most people, I am an on-the-go busy person with a 9-to-5 and family to attend to, so finding time to focus on wellness can be challenging. My goal is to help as many people as possible find the time and resources they need to feel wellness in their lives. Whether I suggest herbal teas, yoga stretches, or specific foods to eat – I am here to provide ideas and knowledge that will support you and your needs.

But…why me? Well, I am a molecular biologist who focuses on the general anatomy of an individual, on top of the biochemical components that make up a person. In short – I have experience looking at things both in the broad, whole-system and the narrow, focused areas. My coursework has included traditional herbalism and evidence-based herbalism, looking at both how people have utilized plants and their compounds to better an individual.

What do I do for you?
  • Come up with a wellness plan to help get you started on your wellness journey.
  • Utilize holistic approaches to ensure all aspects and needs are met for you.
  • Provide resources that will help keep you on track.
  • Formulate teas and products tailored specifically to you and your needs.

The practice I have developed over the years has been especially useful in getting me through hard times and putting me over the moon during the better times. A wellness practice is a way to take charge of what is going on for you and convert all energy – positive, negative, even neutral, stagnant energy – into something that can better your mind, your body, and your emotional well-being. So please – book a consultation today. I am reading to get the waters moving and the good energy flowing.

Looking forward to meeting you at our first consultation today!
